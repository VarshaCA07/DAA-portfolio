SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being

Explanation: This goal is central to all the use cases as they directly contribute to improving healthcare delivery, patient management, and resource allocation, which is vital for good health outcomes. The use of technology in tracking bed availability, prioritizing patients, and coordinating inter-hospital transfers ensures better healthcare services.

Relevant Indicators:

  • Indicator 3.8.1: Coverage of essential health services.
  • Indicator 3.9.1: Mortality rate attributed to chronic diseases (e.g., cardiovascular diseases, cancer).
  • Indicator 3.3.1: Number of new HIV infections per 1,000 uninfected population.
  • Indicator 3.9.2: Mortality rate attributed to unintentional poisoning.
