SDG Contributions in Public Transport Route Optimisation

SDG 9:Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Relevance:

  • Public transport optimization contributes to the development of sustainable, resilient, and inclusive infrastructure by improving connectivity and efficiency.

    • Impact:

  • Uses advanced technologies like AI, IoT, and GPS for real-time route management and traffic optimization.

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being

Relevance:

  • Optimized public transport reduces traffic congestion and air pollution, improving air quality and contributing to better respiratory and cardiovascular health.

    • Impact:

  • Encourages active modes of transport like walking or cycling to transit stops, promoting physical activity.

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

Relevance:

  • Enhances accessibility, affordability, and reliability of public transport systems, reducing private vehicle dependency and urban congestion.

    • Impact:

  • Promotes inclusive mobility for all, including marginalized groups, elderly individuals, and persons with disabilities..
