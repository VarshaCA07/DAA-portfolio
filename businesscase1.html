BUSINESS CASE 1: Managing Traffic in City

1.1. Reducing Economic Losses from Congestion

Problem: Traffic congestion leads to significant economic losses due to delays, fuel wastage, and decreased productivity.

Solution: Implementing advanced traffic management systems (e.g., adaptive traffic signals, real-time traffic monitoring) reduces congestion and optimizes vehicle flow.

Algorithms Used: Binary Search Trees (BST) and Breadth-First Search
