BUSINESS CASE 2: Improved Commuter Productivity and Economic Gains

2.1. Enhanced Workforce Efficiency for Business

Problem: Workers spend substantial time commuting, leading to lost productivity, fatigue, and increased absenteeism.

Solution: By optimizing public transport routes, businesses can reduce the time employees spend on their daily commutes. Public transportation systems can be better synchronized to avoid delays, congestion, and inefficient schedules.

Algorithms Used: Binary Trees, Graph Algorithms, and String Matching Algorithms

2.2. Boost to the Local Economy from More Efficient Workforces

Problem: Traffic congestion and inefficient public transport routes create delays that reduce economic output across all sectors of the economy.

Solution: Optimizing public transport routes improves the flow of commuters, facilitating faster movement of people between work and home. Businesses across various industries can benefit from a more reliable and efficient workforce.

Algorithms Used: Graph Algorithms, Trees, and String Matching Algorithms
